Karage Donburi

Description

The Karage donburi is an excellent japanese main dish consisting of tiny bite sized chunks of fried chicken laid over a bed of rice. The name "Donburi" is japanese for rice bowl and "karage" for fried chicken. Most japanese donburis come with a topping of sea weed and/or red cabbage with a drizzle of spicy japanese mayo on top.

Ingredients

For the Fried Chicken (Karage)

  • 500g boneless chicken pieces (thighs or breasts), cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sake (Japanese rice wine)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup potato starch or cornstarch
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the Donburi Bowl

  • Cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • Sliced green onions
  • Shredded lettuce or cabbage (optional)
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Japanese Spicy mayo

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken
    • In a bowl, combine soy sauce, sake, grated ginger, minced garlic, and sesame oil to create the marinade.
    • Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss to coat. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  2. Coat and Fry the Chicken
    • Remove the marinated chicken from the fridge and let it come to room temperature.
    • Coat the marinated chicken pieces in potato starch or cornstarch, shaking off any excess.
    • In a deep pan or pot, heat vegetable oil to around 170-180Â°C.
    • Carefully fry the coated chicken pieces in batches until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 4-5 minutes per batch. Place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  3. Assemble the Donburi
    • Place a serving of cooked Japanese rice in a bowl.
    • Arrange the fried chicken pieces on top of the rice.
  4. Add Toppings
    • Drizzle some of the japanese spicy mayo over the fried chicken.
    • Scatter your choice of seaweed, sliced green onions, shredded lettuce or cabbage if desired.
  5. Garnish and Serve
    • Sprinkle sesame seeds over the dish for added flavor and presentation.
  6. Enjoy
    • Serve the Karaage Donburi immediately while the chicken is still crispy and hot, along with the savory sauce and tender rice.
